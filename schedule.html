Yoga Flow

Beginner-Intermediate

Mindful yoga practice focusing on breath, movement, and body awareness. Flow through a series of poses designed to improve flexibility, balance, and mental clarity.

⏱️ Duration: 45 minutes
🔥 Intensity: Low to Moderate

Benefits:

  • Improved flexibility and range of motion
  • Enhanced mental relaxation and stress relief
  • Better balance and core stability
  • Increased mind-body connection

HIIT Blast

Advanced

High-intensity interval training combining short bursts of intense exercise with recovery periods. Perfect for burning maximum calories in minimum time.

⏱️ Duration: 30-45 minutes
🔥 Intensity: Very High

  • Improved cardiovascular fitness

  • Effective for weight loss

⏱️ Duration: 50-60 minutes
🔥 Intensity: High

  • Better body composition
  • Improved bone density
  • Enhanced metabolism

Pilates Core

Core strengthening and flexibility exercises using Pilates techniques. Focus on controlled movements to engage deep abdominal muscles and improve posture.

⏱️ Duration: 50 minutes
🔥 Intensity: Moderate

  • Better balance and stability
  • Increased flexibility
