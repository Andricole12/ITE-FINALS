HIIT Blast

High-Intensity Interval Training

Advanced ⚡

High-intensity interval training that combines intense cardio bursts with brief recovery periods, delivering maximum calorie burn and fitness gains in minimum time.

⏱️ Duration: 45 minutes
🎯 Frequency: 3-4x per week
💪 Level: Advanced
📍 Location: Studio A & B

Goals & Benefits:

  • Maximum calorie burn and fat loss
  • Improved cardiovascular endurance
  • Increased metabolic rate (afterburn effect)
  • Time-efficient workouts

What to Expect:

Challenging intervals alternating between all-out effort and recovery periods. Expect to push yourself hard, but modifications will be provided for all fitness levels.

Start HIIT Blast

Yoga Flow

Mindful Movement & Flexibility

Beginner-Intermediate 🌱

Mindful yoga sessions that combine flowing poses, breathwork, and meditation to improve flexibility, balance, reduce stress, and enhance overall wellness.

⏱️ Duration: 50-60 minutes
🎯 Frequency: 2-3x per week
💪 Level: All Levels
📍 Location: Studio C

Goals & Benefits:

  • Enhanced flexibility and range of motion
  • Stress relief and mental clarity
  • Improved balance and core stability
  • Better mind-body connection

What to Expect:

Flowing sequences of poses synchronized with breathing. Bring a mat and wear comfortable clothing. Perfect for beginners and experienced practitioners alike.

Join Yoga Flow

Strength Training

Build Muscle & Power

Intermediate 🏋️

Comprehensive weight training program using progressive overload principles to build muscle mass, increase strength, and improve overall body composition with expert guidance.

⏱️ Duration: 60 minutes
🎯 Frequency: 4-5x per week
💪 Level: Intermediate-Advanced
📍 Location: Weight Room

Goals & Benefits:

  • Significant muscle gain and definition
  • Increased strength and power
  • Improved bone density and metabolism
  • Better posture and injury prevention

What to Expect:

Structured workouts using barbells, dumbbells, and machines. Each session includes warm-up, main lifts, and accessory exercises. Form coaching included.

Start Strength Training

Pilates Core

Core Strength & Control

Beginner to Advanced 🌟

Core-strengthening program using Pilates principles to develop functional strength, improve posture, increase flexibility, and enhance muscular control throughout the body.

⏱️ Duration: 50 minutes
🎯 Frequency: 3x per week
💪 Level: All Levels
📍 Location: Studio D

Goals & Benefits:

  • Strong, defined core muscles
  • Improved posture and alignment
  • Better balance and stability
  • Increased flexibility and body awareness

What to Expect:

Controlled, precise movements focusing on the core. Classes use mats and props. Movements are low-impact but highly effective. Great for injury recovery.

Start Pilates Core

Cardio Dance

Dance, Burn, Groove

Beginner 🎉

Fun, energetic dance-based cardio workout that burns calories, improves coordination, and boosts mood while dancing to favorite music in a supportive group environment.

⏱️ Duration: 45 minutes
🎯 Frequency: 2-3x per week
💪 Level: All Levels
📍 Location: Studio E

Goals & Benefits:

  • Excellent cardiovascular workout
  • Improved coordination and rhythm
  • Effective calorie burn in a fun way
  • Mood boost and stress relief

Boxing Fitness

Power, Speed & Confidence

Intermediate 🏆

Non-contact boxing workout combining punching combinations, footwork, and cardio to build endurance, coordination, power, and provide excellent stress relief in a supportive environment.

⏱️ Duration: 55 minutes
🎯 Frequency: 3-4x per week
💪 Level: Intermediate
📍 Location: Ring Studio

Goals & Benefits:

  • Full-body cardiovascular workout
  • Improved hand-eye coordination
  • Excellent stress and tension relief
  • Increased confidence and mental toughness

What to Expect:

Learn proper boxing technique, combinations, and footwork. Use heavy bags and pads. Get gloves and hand wraps provided. Intense but incredibly rewarding workout.

Start Boxing Fitness
