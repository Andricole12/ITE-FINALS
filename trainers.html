❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right trainer for me?

+

Consider your fitness goals and preferred training style. We recommend reading trainer profiles and watching introduction videos. You can also schedule a free consultation with a trainer to discuss your goals before committing.

What are your trainer pricing options?

+

We offer flexible pricing: Single Session: ₱500, 5-Session Package: ₱2,250 (10% discount), 10-Session Package: ₱4,200 (16% discount), Monthly Unlimited: ₱8,000. Group sessions are also available at reduced rates.

Can I switch trainers?

+

Absolutely! We want you to be comfortable with your trainer. If you feel you're not connecting well after your first few sessions, you can switch to another trainer at no extra cost. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Do trainers offer customized workout plans?

+

Yes! All our trainers create personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and any injuries or limitations. They assess you during the first session and adjust the plan as you progress.

What certifications do your trainers have?

+

All our trainers are certified by recognized organizations such as NASM, ACE, ISSA, or equivalent. They receive ongoing professional development and stay updated with the latest fitness trends and safety protocols.

How often should I train with a personal trainer?

+

This depends on your goals and current fitness level. Most beginners benefit from 2-3 sessions per week, while advanced clients may do 4-5. Your trainer will recommend a schedule that fits your goals and lifestyle.

Can trainers help with nutrition advice?

+

Yes! Our trainers can provide general nutrition guidance and healthy eating tips. For specialized nutrition plans, we can refer you to our certified nutritionists. Nutrition is included in our Premium membership.

How do I book a session with a trainer?

+

You can book sessions directly through our website, call us at +1-800-GYM-STAMINA, or visit us in person. First-time clients get a free 15-minute consultation to discuss goals and expectations before their first session.

What's your cancellation policy?

+

We require 24-hour notice to cancel or reschedule sessions. Cancellations made with less notice will be charged. However, packages expire after 6 months if unused, so plan accordingly.
